<sub> and <sup> tags are used for subscript and superscript. 

<body>
  Water = H<sub>2</sub>O
  <br/>
  1000 = 10<sup>3</sup>
</body>

Inline styling using style attribute.

<body style="background-color:yellow;">
  <img src="images/wncc.png" width="100" height="40"/>
  <p style="color:red;margin-left:20px;">This is a red colored paragraph.</p>
</body>
